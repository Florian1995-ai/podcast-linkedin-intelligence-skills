# LinkedIn Candidate Scoring

A wrong LinkedIn URL is worse than a blank one. High confidence needs strong identity evidence: slug contains first and last name, result text repeats the exact name, and title/snippet agrees with podcast, company, niche, role, or location context.

Mark `verify` when the name matches but context is weak. Mark `low` for partial names, unrelated same-name results, generic titles, or company pages.

Never accept `/company/`, `/school/`, `/jobs/`, `/posts/`, `/pulse/`, `/learning/`, or `/dir/` as person profiles.
